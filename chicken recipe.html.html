Popcorn chicken recipe

       

Serving 4

               

This is a quick example html project building popcorn chicken recipe

       
       

Ingredients

       
               
  • 2 chicken breasts
    •            
  • 2 cups buttermilk
    •            
  • 1 packet ranch dressing mix
    •            
  • 2 cups cheese crackers
    •            
  • Oil for frying
    •        
               
               

Preparation

       
               
  1. Chop chicken breasts into bite size pieces.
    2.            
  2. In a large bowl, combine chicken,buttermilk and ranch packet,and refrigerate for 2 hours.
    3.            
  3. In a large ziplock bag,cruble the cheese crackers into desired crumbs.
    4.            
  4. Place cracker crumbs into a medium bowl and coat each piece of chicken until fully covered.
    5.            
  5. Heat oil to 350 degrees ferenheit and fry chicken until crackers turn golden brown(3 minutes).
    6.            
  6. Place on a paper towel to dry.
    7.            
  7. Enjoy with a side of ranch or any other sauce!
    8.
